🧠 CBT Techniques

Cognitive Restructuring

Identify negative thoughts → Examine evidence → Create balanced thoughts. Challenge "all-or-nothing" thinking patterns.

Behavioral Activation

Schedule enjoyable activities even when unmotivated. Start small: a 5-minute walk, calling a friend, or making your bed.

Thought Records

Write down: Situation → Automatic Thought → Emotion → Evidence For/Against → Balanced Thought → New Feeling.

Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)

Name 5 things you see, 4 you touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Brings you back to the present.

💡 Daily Wellness Tips

Sleep Hygiene

Consistent schedule, no screens 30min before bed, cool room (65-68°F), avoid caffeine after 2pm.

Mindful Movement

Even 10 minutes of walking, stretching, or yoga can significantly reduce stress and improve mood.

Social Connection

Reach out to one person daily. Quality matters more than quantity — even a brief check-in helps.

Nutrition & Hydration

Eat regular meals. Omega-3s, whole grains, and leafy greens support mental health. Stay hydrated.
